What does Yooply do?

Yooply makes discipline management easier and more effective. Admins love that it cuts down on paperwork and centralizes all the behavior data, from student statements to detention minutes.

Teachers can quickly send their referrals straight from their smartphones and keep track of discipline outcomes. No more wondering where their students went.

Students can record their incident reports, write reflections, and give each other positive feedback, all from their mobiles, an office computer, or any device with an internet browser.

Discipline shouldn't eat up the school day

Admins shouldn't have to spend all day managing behavior and paperwork, and students shouldn't waste instructional time sitting in the office.

Put your school's discipline plan in everyone's hand

Yooply is not about adopting a whole new discipline plan. It's about making yours more meaningful and easier to manage. You can customize the experience for your students and staff and align it to your model, including specific behaviors and reflection questions.

Discipline Matters

The poor management of school discipline is one of two main reasons teachers leave their schools. Students and teachers consistently rate discipline issues as a major obstacle to effective learning. When behavior systems are ineffective or poorly enforced, it makes it hard to create a positive and effective learning environment.

No more paperwork

Admins can also manage and track detention minutes, student contracts, suspensions, notify parents and keep all teachers informed. No more clipboards, no more clunky spreadsheets or desktop software everyone hates.

Real time data

Keeping track of student data and discipline trends is easy. Teachers, admins, parents and students can monitor school behavior stats and make better decisions that ultimately impact learning.

Let's stay positive!

With Yooply, students can send each other positive messages, earn points and rewards, unlock features and monitor their own behavior.

Customize

Schools can tailor Yooply to fit their discipline plans. Add your own consequences, rewards, and discipline tags to match your state, district, or school requirements.

Interventions

Keep track of interventions, from verbal redirections to buddy teacher referral. Why? Because teachers, counselors and admins should have the option to track minor infractions to analyze patterns of behaviors before they become more serious.

Integrate with your own SIS

With Yooply student data can be imported from major Student Information Systems. Keep your system up-to-date with regular syncing. Staff and students can quickly log in with their Google Accounts.
